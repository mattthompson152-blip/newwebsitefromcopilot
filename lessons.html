What You'll Learn

Each lesson is tailored to your voice, goals, and experience level.

Vocal Technique

Breath control, tone, resonance, pitch, and agility.

Range Expansion

Build strength, flexibility, and control across registers.

Performance Skills

Confidence, expression, musicality, and stage presence.

Song Interpretation

Phrasing, emotion, style, and storytelling.

Recording Technique

Microphone skills and consistent tone for studio work.

Artist Development

Find your unique voice and build your creative identity.

Lesson Structure

Every session follows a clear structure to maximise your progress.

1. Vocal Warm-Up

Prepare your voice with targeted exercises.

2. Technique Work

Build skills through focused exercises and coaching.

3. Song Application

Apply techniques to songs you choose with guidance.

4. Feedback & Goals

Review progress and set targets for next time.
